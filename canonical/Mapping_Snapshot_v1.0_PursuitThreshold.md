# Parameter Mapping Snapshot v1.0

## Pursuit Threshold

Status: Frozen / Authoritative\
Layer: Parameter Mapping Layer\
Anchor: Latest Archetype Version

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# I. Parameter Identity

Pursuit Threshold\
Domain: \[1--10\] (normalized internally to \[0,1\])

Definition (Canonical): Controls the structural confirmation level
required before committing to deep pursuit of a collapsing enemy.

Pursuit Threshold does NOT directly modify: - Damage - HitProbability -
Speed (base) - Risk Appetite - Offense / Defense Weight - Targeting
Logic - Time Preference - Formation Rigidity

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# II. Core Interpretation

Pursuit Threshold defines:

The minimum confirmed structural collapse signal required to initiate
deep pursuit.

Low Threshold → aggressive pursuit\
High Threshold → conservative confirmation before pursuit

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# III. Functional Role in Sandbox Engine

Let:

EnemyCollapseSignal = 1 - EnemyCohesion

Deep pursuit condition:

If EnemyCollapseSignal \> PursuitThreshold: EngageDeepPursuit()

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# IV. Structural Consequences of Deep Pursuit

Deep pursuit modifies structural conditions indirectly:

-   Increases exposure (extended front)
-   Increases formation stretch
-   Reduces structural compactness
-   Reduces reconfiguration stability

Deep pursuit does NOT directly modify Cohesion value.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# V. Trade-off Structure

Low Pursuit Threshold:

-   Faster exploitation of enemy collapse\
-   Potential decisive elimination\
    − Higher overextension risk\
    − Greater vulnerability to counterattack

High Pursuit Threshold:

-   Structural safety\
-   Maintained formation integrity\
    − Lost elimination opportunity\
    − Longer battle duration

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# VI. Causal Chain (Non-Shortcut Rule)

Pursuit Threshold → DeepPursuit Decision\
DeepPursuit Decision → Structural Stretch\
Structural Stretch → Exposure Variation\
Exposure Variation → Cohesion Dynamics\
Cohesion Dynamics → Breakthrough or Stabilization

Direct modification of Cohesion is prohibited.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# VII. Orthogonality Constraint

Pursuit Threshold must remain orthogonal to:

-   Risk Appetite (uncertainty tolerance)
-   Offense / Defense Weight (engagement posture)
-   Mobility Bias (movement capability)
-   Formation Rigidity (structural elasticity)
-   Perception Radius (information coverage)
-   Targeting Logic (target evaluation)

It governs post-collapse exploitation behavior only.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
